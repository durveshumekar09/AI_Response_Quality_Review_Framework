## S21 | Comparison Question
**User Query:** Which is better: short-term or long-term loan?
**Prompt V1 Response:**
It depends on your goal and cash flow.

Short-term loan is usually better when:
- you can afford higher monthly payments
- you want to finish repayment quickly
- you want to pay less total interest overall

Long-term loan is usually better when:
- you need lower monthly EMIs
- the loan amount is large
- you want more breathing room in your budget
- you can accept paying more total interest over time

Main trade-off:
- Short-term: higher EMI, lower total interest
- Long-term: lower EMI, higher total interest

If you share loan amount, monthly income, and existing EMIs, I can suggest what fits better.
